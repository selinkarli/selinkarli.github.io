PRODUCER PROFILE

Born in Istanbul, Selin Karli received her BA in Communications from Pepperdine University in Malibu, California. She started her career at ANS Productions as a Festivals Coordinator and later worked as a director’s assistant for TV productions and commercials.

After receiving her MFA in Producing from the American Film Institute Conservatory (AFI) in Los Angeles, California, she took different positions in various production companies in the USA.

She produced short films, both in Turkey and the USA.

Selin previously worked as an International Sales and Festivals Coordinator at Bulut Film as well as at freelance.

Selin worked with producers such as Funda Odemis on BROTHERS (Dir: Ömür Atay) and Zeynep Atakan on THE WILD PEAR TREE (Dir: Nuri Bilge Ceylan) as an international production coordinator.

Since 2014, Selin has been the Project Manager for NEW TURKISH FILMS, which aims to promote Turkish Cinema around some of world’s most important film festivals for the Film Producer’s Association of Turkey (Se-Yap). For this project, she has collaborated with representatives from Cannes, Berlin, Venice, Toronto, Sundance, Locarno, Rotterdam and San Sebastian Film Festivals.

Selin was previously selected to attend Rotterdam Lab (2015), Talents Sarajevo (2016), EAVE Marketing Workshop (2017) and LIM Less is More (Story Editor, 2018).

She has been developing her mini-series called THE ORIGIN OF ANOTHER WORLD since her attendance to Torino Film Lab, TV Series Extended 2018. She is also working on a short memoir of her road-trip in the US called SOUL SEARCHING THROUGH DELTA BLUES.
